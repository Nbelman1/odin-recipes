Tuna Casserole

Description

A proper tuna casserole has the cheese cooked to a light brown color. While optional, French-fried onions add quite the tasty topping. Green peas add a balance of sweetness to the mix.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag egg noodles
  • 2 10.5 oz cans condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 block of cheddar (just not that white cheddar, that shit nasty), shredded
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup frozen corn (unless your girlfriend hates corn)
  • 1 package cremini mushrooms, freshly sliced
  • 2 5 oz cans tuna, drained

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Al dente is the MVP here.
  3. Combine the cream of mushroom soup, cheddar, peas, corn, mushrooms, tuna, and half of the cheddar in a large mixing bowl. Resist the temptation to try the concoction.
  4. Drain the egg noodles and add them to the mixture. With a large spoon, mix until your arm hurts.
  5. Pour the whole bowl of deliciousness into a casserole dish and top with the remaining cheddar, then a healthy dose of French-fried onions.
  6. Cook for 20 minutes or until cheese starts to brown. Remove from oven, preferably using oven mitts.
  7. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving. Bask in the gloriousness of your family showering you with compliments.
